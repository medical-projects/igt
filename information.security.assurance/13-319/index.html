Information Security Assurance

11-319

There are documented plans and procedures to support business continuity in the event of power failures, system failures, natural disasters and other disruptions

Level 1.

a.

There has been an assessment of the risks to all systems where information critical to the running of the organisation is held which has been documented.

a.
